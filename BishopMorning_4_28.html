Greek Forces🔗

Vanna: partially discharged from yesterday. VIX compressed 20.78 → 19.95 through the session yesterday and dealer buying confirmed into close. That move was the vanna spring releasing. Charm: building into FOMC. Wednesday's FOMC creates two distinct charm phases. Pre-announcement: dealers continue to hedge for tail risk in both directions, charm decay is slow. Post-announcement (assuming uneventful): rapid charm decay forces dealers to unwind hedges, creating mechanical buying pressure into Wed PM earnings. The 5 mega-cap names reporting Wed PM amplify this — implied moves are pricing 4–6% per name (MSFT ±$25, GOOGL ±$17, META ±$43, AMZN ±$16, QCOM ±$11).

Net delta: Dealer book exited yesterday at the +$1.21B GEX level with positive net delta. The exposure is asymmetric — defending the structure costs less than breaking it. That's part of why retracements within positive gamma regimes tend to hold absent a structural catalyst. Today's question is whether the OpenAI miss + WTI breakout = structural catalyst, or just heavy noise that gets absorbed.
