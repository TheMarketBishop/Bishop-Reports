Glossary

Quick reference — key terms used in this report.

GEX
Gamma Exposure — net dealer gamma; negative = dealers sell into dips (amplifying)
Accelerator
Strike with large negative gamma — price approaching it triggers forced dealer selling, creating cascades
Vanna
Delta sensitivity to IV — when vol drops, positive vanna forces dealers to buy aggressively
OpEx
Options Expiration — tomorrow Apr 24. 901K puts expire. If above $700 at close, puts die and provide relief
NPP/NCP
Net Put Premium / Net Call Premium — the dollar flow into puts vs calls. NPP dominating = bearish positioning
Dispersion
Cross-sectional standard deviation — measures how much individual stocks scatter vs the index. Extreme = stock-picking regime
