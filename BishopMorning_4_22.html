Quick Reference — Key Terms Used In This Report

GEX (Gamma Exposure)
Aggregate dealer gamma. Positive = dealers buy dips, stabilize. Negative = dealers amplify every move.
GEX Flip
Price where GEX crosses zero. Above = cushion. Below = every move overshoots. Currently 7,055.

How delta changes when IV changes. +69.6K (bullish). Vol crush forces automatic dealer share buying.
