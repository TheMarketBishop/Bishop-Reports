Glossary

Quick reference — key terms used in this report.

GEX
Gamma Exposure — net dealer gamma positioning; positive = dealers buy dips / sell rips (stabilizing)
GEX Flip
Strike where dealer gamma flips from positive to negative — the structural floor
Magnet
Strike with high positive gamma — price tends to drift toward and pin at magnets
Vanna
Sensitivity of delta to implied volatility — when IV falls, positive vanna forces dealers to buy
Charm
Time-decay of delta — as options near expiry, charm adjustments force dealer hedging flows
Block Tape
Institutional trades >$500K — net flow shows whether large players are buying or selling
A/D Ratio
Advance/Decline ratio — measures breadth; above 1.0 = more stocks rising than falling
Power Trend
Technical trigger: 21 EMA > 50 SMA, price above EMA 10+ days, SMA trending up, close green
Composite Score
Weighted aggregate of volatility, breadth, momentum, bonds, options, and market data metrics
